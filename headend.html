Multichannel H.264 high-definition Encoder GN-1846

The GN-1846 is a multichannel H.264 high-definition encoder, It can perform up to 4 high-definition and low-bit-rate audio and video encoding functions on a single board. Support 4-channel audio and video coding, encoded audio and video ES packet by TS flow through an IP channel to UDP output, in device management, support Web network management, and support for online control program upgrades.

High cost-performance HD/SD video Transcoder GN-1868

GN-1868 is a high cost-performance HD/SD video transcoder.


It is capable of transcoding multiple channels MPEG-2/H.264 HD/SD video to HEVC(H.265), Dolby digital audio passthrough and transcoding MPEG-1 Layer II/AAC/Dolby audio to MPEG-1 Layer II/AAC digital audio.

GOSPELL GN-1738 transcoder

GOSPELL GN-1738 transcoder is highly competitive SD video conversion unit with best-in-class video quality.

It can convert up to eight channels of MPEG-2 (or MPEG-4 AVC) SD live video streams to MPEG-4 AVC (or MPEG-2) video formats.

Gospell’s GN-1858 Encoder

Gospell’s GN-1858 is a new generation, cost-effective MPEG-2 and MPGE-4 AVC SD encoder with broadcast-quality. It’s ideal for broadcasters, cable network operators and media providers looking to reduce equipment costs for digital television distribution application.

GOSPELL’s GC-1816 IRD

GOSPELL’s GC-1816 is a cost effective and professional integrated receiver and decoder (IRD), and was designed to receive and descramble digital scrambled program streams from satellite or cable network and ASI interface.

Multi-Functional IPQAM GQ-3655

GQ-3655 is the GOSPELL’s latest multi-functional IPQAM product series, which can receive multiple program sources from cable (or satellite, DTT network), IP network and local ASI, and then re-multiplexing, scrambling and modulating it with channel coding.

IP-Based Stream Multiplexer and Scrambler

IP-Based Stream Multiplexer and Scrambler With IP input & Output The GM-8000A has a 1RU, highly integrated and fully IP- based design that can reduce rack space and power consumption. It can also greatly simplify the headend infrastructure for the service provider. Moreover, the GM-8000A supports Web browser management for convenient management and maintenance. With this stream processing platform, service providers will have a scalable,
reliable and highly competitive video stream solution for subscribers with lower total cost of ownership.

GOSPELL Conditional Access System (CAS)

The GOSPELL Conditional Access System (CAS) is a security enhanced pay TV solution, and was designed to provide a completely secure media environment for operator content. with GOSPELL CAS, service providers can be sure that they can offer security enhanced content protection, allow them to increase Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) from subscribers, but with a low cost of ownership.It can encrypt control words and user authorization information within a stable and highly secured environment, and fully compatible with DVB Simul-Crypt standard.
