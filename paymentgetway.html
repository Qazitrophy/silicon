SILICON PayNow Payment Getway

SILICON PayNow is the one of the first payment gateway in Bangladesh opening doors for merchants to receive payments on the internet via their online stores. Customers are able to buy products online using their credit cards as well as bank accounts. If you are a merchant, you have come to the right place! Enhance your business by integrating SILICON PayNow to your online store and facilitating online payment in Bangladeshi Taka. Your customers will be able to pay for your products using local credit/debit cards like VISA, MasterCard, DBBL Nexus Card and any kind of credit card or bank accounts and all MFS like bkash, Rocket, Nogod, Telecash etc. right from your online store. SILICON PayNow uses industry standard Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology which is used worldwide for securing data encryption. There are deferents ways to display the SILICON PayNow payment page for your customer.
